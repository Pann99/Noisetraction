What is Noisetraction?

Noisetraction brings you a unique way to experience calm and concentration.

Here, you can explore a blend of rain, forest, ocean, and other natural ambiances with the beautiful tones of traditional Indonesian instruments and timeless classics.

Every sound can be adjusted and combined to match your personal rhythm, making it your own space for focus and relaxation.








Noisetraction for Work

Noise can easily interrupt your focus and drain your energy.

With Noisetraction, you can mask distractions using brown noise, traditional instruments, or calming nature sounds. Adjust and combine them freely to create the atmosphere that keeps you focused and motivated.

Use the built-in timer to structure your workflow, reduce stress, and maintain steady productivity throughout the day.








Noisetraction for Study

Studying requires the right atmosphere to stay concentrated and stress-free.

Noisetraction lets you enter study mode more easily with sounds that keep your mind calm and engaged, from gentle rain to soothing instruments.

Set up your preferred mix and combine it with a timer to structure your study sessions better. With the right soundscape, you can focus longer, retain more, and avoid unnecessary distractions.








Noisetraction for Sleep

Falling asleep isn't always easy.

Noise, stress, or even silence can keep your mind awake. Noisetraction offers soothing soundscapes that guide you into deeper relaxation.

From gentle rain and flowing water to calming instruments, you can build the perfect atmosphere for rest. Let the sounds ease your thoughts and help you drift into a peaceful night's sleep.
